Project Overview

About the Project

HazardWatch Rwanda is a digital platform built to strengthen climate resilience and disaster risk management across Rwanda. It predicts, monitors, and communicates risks such as floods, droughts, and landslides — empowering local communities, sector leaders, and national agencies to act quickly and effectively.

Aim

The platform’s primary aim is to reduce vulnerability and save lives by delivering timely, localized, and actionable information about climate hazards.

How It Works

Predict & Monitor

Uses sensor data, satellite imagery, and forecasts to track hazards.

Visualize Risks

Interactive maps show hazard-prone zones at all administrative levels.

Send Alerts

Delivers early warnings via SMS and mobile notifications.

Empower Communities

Provides checklists, planning resources, and quick actions.

Why It’s Unique

HazardWatch Rwanda is community-centered. Unlike generic hazard dashboards, it tailors alerts and resources to Rwanda’s geography and governance model. It ensures inclusivity with offline-friendly outputs (SMS, printable checklists, exportable reports), so even remote communities can benefit.
